## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Locally on your device (IndexedDB). No cloud, no tracking.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**
A: Yes! Especially if installed as PWA.

**Q: Will I lose data if I clear browser cache?**
A: Yes! Always keep backups using the export feature.

**Q: Can I sync across devices?**
A: Not automatically. Use export/import to manually sync.

**Q: Why some tasks show "in Xm"?**
A: That's the countdown to when the task starts!
